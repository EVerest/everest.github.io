##################
dc_external_derate
##################

Power supply DC types

.. _dc_external_derate-ExternalDerating:

| **ExternalDerating**:*object* 
|  Derate DC power supply Capabilities from an external source.
|  properties:
|   **max_export_current_A**:*number* <required>
|    Maximum current that the power supply can output in Ampere
|   **max_import_current_A**:*number* <required>
|    Maximum current that the power supply can sink in Ampere
|   **max_export_power_W**:*number* <required>
|    Maximum export power that the power supply can output in Watt
|   **max_import_power_W**:*number* <required>
|    Maximum import power that the power supply can sink in Watt
