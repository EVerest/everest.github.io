JsEnergyManager¶

Description¶

This modules provides Smart Charging

Provides¶

main:energy_manager
This is the pid_controller interface of the module EnergyManager, it provides config options
config:
pid_d_weight:number
Weight of derivative term
pid_i_limit:number
Limit of integral term
pid_i_weight:number
Weight of integral term
pid_max_output:number
Maximum of output power in watts
pid_min_output:number
Minimum of output power in watts
pid_output_interval:integer
Output interval in milliseconds
pid_p_weight:number
Weight of proportional term
pid_setpoint:number
Setpoint of the PID-Controller

Requirements¶

chargingdriverenergy:evse_manager_energy_control
chargingdriverpowermeter:powermeter
gridpowermeter:powermeter
